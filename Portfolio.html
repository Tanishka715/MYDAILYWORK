About Me

Hello, I’m Tanishka Katkar, a Computer Engineering student at PDEA College of Engineering, where I’m thrilled to have achieved a perfect SGPA of 10 in First year. My current focus is on full-stack development, and I’m proficient in C, C++, and Python. Alongside my technical pursuits, I’m a dedicated Linux user and a robotics teacher at a primary school, where I share my passion for technology with young students. My enthusiasm for robotics is further expressed through my participation in line follower competitions.

In addition to my academic and professional interests, I lead an active and diverse life. I enjoy swimming, cooking, and participating in sports such as volleyball and basketball. These activities not only keep me physically fit but also teach me valuable lessons in teamwork and perseverance. As a national-level dancer, I bring a sense of creativity and discipline to everything I do. My aim is to explore various fields and continuously develop my skills, with a goal of becoming a well-rounded professional. Regardless of the challenge, I am committed to giving my best, driven by dedication and consistency.
